Items

In AutoAssist, we are all about letting you control your items. This means when an update occurs, it will never force items into your collection. Unfortunately, this also means that you will not get new style updates unless you add them yourself. As such, below are links that you can open in AutoAssist to add the items, so you do not have to type it all out yourself. To use these links, just open them on your device, and when you are prompted to Open in "AutoAssist" tap Open. The add items screen will appear in AutoAssist with the information filled out for you. If you like it, just tap Add!
